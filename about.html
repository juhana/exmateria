Ex Materia

Ex Materia was written by Juhana Leinonen for the Eleventh Hour New Year's Minicomp in January 4th, 2018.

Ex Materia displays content from Wikipedia based on the player's input. The author is not responsible for the external content.

Full source code is available at https://github.com/juhana/exmateria.

Privacy policy

At one point in the story you're asked to type a search query. The search query is saved on the server for statistics and dynamic story content. No other information apart from the search query is saved. Some saved search queries might be shown to other players during their playthrough.

Ex Materia uses Google Analytics for analyzing visitor data. Google's privacy policy applies. Google Analytics data cannot be linked to collected search queries.
